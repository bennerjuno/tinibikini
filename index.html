Why Tini Bikini

The problem
we're solving.

Standard swimwear is built around an average that doesn't include everyone. Smaller-chested women have been told to buy the smallest size and make it work — pinning the back, stuffing the cups, returning yet another top that gaps.

Tini Bikini was built to fix that. Every cup, every strap, every seam starts with a smaller bust in mind.

01
Petite cups, by design
Smaller triangles, set closer together, positioned higher. No gaping. No excess fabric.
02
No padding. No underwire.
You don't need to be "corrected." Tini fits the way you are, not the way you're supposed to be.
03
Luxury details, real fit
Scallop lace, satin finishes, ruching — the kind of swimwear that photographs beautifully and fits even better.
04
Made for her.
Every decision — from cup angle to strap width — is made with one customer in mind. You.
